Classical composers homework

Oct 10, 2025

I wanted some homework to listen my way through a reasonable list of classical composers. I don't get it by osmosis like I did in grad school when I listened to classical radio, nor do I wish to. But I want to learn about and be exposed to more than I know now. I decided what I wanted was a list of a couple hundred composers, and then I'd dig into their works by reading about them and seeing what Dave Hurwitz said about them on YouTube and so on. Using Classical Archives, Apple Music, and Wikipedia, I've created the following table.
